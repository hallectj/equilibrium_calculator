How This Calculator Works

This calculator computes the equilibrium value. The equilibrium is the amount of mass needed to make the balloon neither float or sink. The calculator makes the following assumptions

  • The density of helium is 0.179kg/m3
  • The density of air is 1.225kg/m3
  • The acceleration due to gravity is 9.8m/s2
  • The balloons are treated as spheres when determining volume.
